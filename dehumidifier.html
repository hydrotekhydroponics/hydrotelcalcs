Dehumidifier Sizing Calculator

When planning an indoor greenhouse, it’s crucial to consider the amount of moisture that will build up as a result of plant transpiration.

Dehumidifier size must be determined, and this will depend on your grow room’s specific needs.

A high-quality dehumidifier ensures optimal humidity levels, which are crucial for maintaining the air quality in a grow room.

Calculating Your Grow Room’s Dehumidification Needs

To correctly size a dehumidifier for your indoor grow room, consider the specific environment you’ll be working in and the daily operations.

  • How much water are you giving your plants daily?
  • Are you running an HVAC system?
  • What is your runoff percentage?
